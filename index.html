How to open a shop with us

  • Create an account on Trisshik.
  • Prepare high-quality images and detailed descriptions of your items.
  • Set reasonable prices for your preloved clothing items.
  • List your items in appropriate categories or tags.
  • Provide accurate information about the condition, size, and brand of each item.
  • Respond promptly to inquiries and messages from potential buyers.
  • Regularly update your shop with new inventory to keep it fresh and engaging.

Commission

A commission fee of 5% will only be applied once your item is sold and has found a satisfied new owner.

Delivery

Each seller has the flexibility to decide their preferred method for shipping their items. One available choice involves utilizing a partnered delivery service like Fargo, 2nk or Dreamline with the associated cost being automatically included during the checkout process.
